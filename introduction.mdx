# Welcome to Plotlist

Plotlist is a **TV-first social tracking and discovery app** built with Expo, React Native, and Convex. It's designed as a social companion for television, helping users track what they're watching, connect with friends, and discover their next favorite show.

<Note>
  Plotlist is exclusively focused on TV shows and episodes - not movies. The entire catalog and recommendation system is optimized around television content.
</Note>

## What is Plotlist?

Plotlist combines personal tracking, social discovery, and release planning into a unified experience for TV enthusiasts. Whether you're managing your watchlist, logging episodes, writing reviews, or discovering shows through friends, Plotlist provides the tools you need.

## Key Features

### Tracking & Logging

- **Watch States**: Track shows across `watchlist`, `watching`, `completed`, and `dropped`
- **Episode Logging**: Log watched episodes with optional notes and timestamps
- **Progress Tracking**: Automatically track your progress through seasons and episodes
- **Ratings & Reviews**: Rate and review shows or individual episodes

### Social Features

- **User Profiles**: Public profiles with follower/following counts, watch stats, and curated lists
- **Activity Feed**: Personalized home feed showing reviews and watch logs from followed users
- **Lists**: Create ranked public or private lists of shows with cover images
- **Engagement**: Like, comment on, and share user-generated content
- **Contact Discovery**: Find friends through privacy-preserving contact sync with hashed phone numbers

### Discovery & Recommendations

- **Personalized Rails**: Trending shows, most-reviewed content, and provider-specific recommendations
- **AI Recommendations**: Taste-based suggestions generated from embeddings and preference signals
- **Similar Shows**: Discover shows similar to ones you love
- **Semantic Search**: Search with freeform prompts like "comfort comedy" or "slow-burn sci-fi"
- **Provider Filtering**: Browse content from Netflix, Apple TV, Hulu, Disney+, Max, and Prime Video

### Release Planning

- **Release Calendar**: View upcoming episodes from your tracked shows
- **Filtered Views**: See tonight's episodes, premieres, returning seasons, and finales
- **Provider Filtering**: Filter calendar by streaming service
- **Background Sync**: Automatic refresh and caching of release data

## Tech Stack

### Frontend

- **Expo + React Native**: Cross-platform mobile development
- **Expo Router**: File-based routing and navigation
- **NativeWind**: Tailwind CSS for React Native
- **React Native Reanimated**: Smooth animations and interactions
- **FlashList**: High-performance lists for large feeds

### Backend

- **Convex**: Realtime database, queries, mutations, actions, and cron jobs
- **Convex Auth**: Phone number authentication with Twilio Verify OTP
- **TMDB**: External show metadata, search, and release data
- **Gemini**: Embeddings for personalized recommendations and semantic search

## Core Data Model

Plotlist's backend is built on Convex with the following key domains:

- **Users**: Profiles, onboarding, visibility settings, and account management
- **Shows**: TMDB ingestion, catalog search, metadata caching, and provider links
- **Watch States**: User tracking states (watchlist, watching, completed, dropped)
- **Watch Logs**: Episode and show watch history with timestamps and notes
- **Reviews**: User reviews with ratings, likes, and comments
- **Lists**: User-curated collections with drag-and-drop reordering
- **Feed**: Social activity feed aggregating reviews and logs
- **Release Calendar**: Cached future episode data and personalized calendar views
- **Embeddings**: Taste profiles, recommendation rails, and semantic search
